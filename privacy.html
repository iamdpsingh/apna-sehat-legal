Apna Sehat – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04 February 2026

Medical Disclaimer:
Apna Sehat is a digital health and telemedicine support application. It provides preliminary health triage and informational guidance only. It does not provide a medical diagnosis, treatment, or replace consultation with a qualified healthcare professional. In case of a medical emergency, contact local emergency services immediately.

1. Information We Collect

To provide core telemedicine and triage functionality, Apna Sehat may collect:

  • Symptoms and health-related inputs entered by you
  • Triage results such as risk zone (Green / Yellow / Red) and guidance
  • Basic metadata including timestamps and app usage context
  • Optional identification details (only when online features are enabled)

2. On-Device and Offline Usage

Apna Sehat is designed to work in offline mode. By default, all health data is stored locally on your device. No internet connection or account login is required to access core triage features.

3. Online Sync & Telemedicine Features (Phase 2)

When online features are enabled, the app may securely sync selected data to our servers so that authorized medical professionals or healthcare systems can access it for review.

  • Online sync occurs only when internet is available
  • Authentication may use mobile number verification (OTP)
  • Offline functionality remains available at all times

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate health triage and safety guidance
  • To maintain your medical case history
  • To securely share information with healthcare providers (if enabled)
  • To improve app reliability, safety, and performance

5. Data Sharing

We respect your privacy. Apna Sehat does not sell personal or health data.

  • No advertising or marketing data sharing
  • No third-party access without your consent
  • Medical data is shared only for healthcare purposes

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information, including secure storage and controlled access. However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • You can delete local data from within the app or by uninstalling it
  • If online sync is enabled, you may request deletion of server-stored data
  • Deleted data is permanently removed from our systems

8. Children’s Privacy

Apna Sehat is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us for removal.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available within the app and at this page.
