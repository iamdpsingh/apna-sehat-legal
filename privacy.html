Apna Sehat – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-04

Important: Apna Sehat provides informational triage assistance only. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical advice. For emergencies, call local emergency services.

1. What data we collect

  • Symptoms text you enter
  • Triage output (zone classification, informational guidance, and metadata such as confidence if available)
  • Timestamps and locally stored case history

2. Where your data is stored

By default, your data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. In Phase 2 (online mode), the app may sync your data to our server when internet is available and you choose to verify/sign in.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide triage assistance and maintain your case history
  • To sync your case history securely when online (Phase 2)
  • To improve reliability and safety of the app (aggregate, non-identifying analytics if enabled later)

4. Sharing and selling

We do not sell your health data. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising.

5. Online features and authentication (Phase 2)

When online features are enabled, the app may use mobile number verification (OTP) and authentication tokens to secure your account and enable syncing. You can still use core triage features offline.

6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Data retention and deletion

  • You can delete your local app data from within the app (if provided) or by uninstalling the app.
  • If online sync is enabled, you may request deletion of server-stored data by contacting us.

8. Children’s privacy

Apna Sehat is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
